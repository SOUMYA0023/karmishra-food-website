Our Legacy

Since 1985

The Story of Madan Mohan Kar & Sarojini Mishra

Founded by the culinary genius of Madan Mohan Kar and the hospitality wisdom of Sarojini Mishra, KarMishra RuchiSeva began as a small kitchen serving authentic Odia meals to neighbors and community events.

What started as a passion for preserving traditional Odia flavors has grown into one of the most trusted catering services in the state, while maintaining the same homemade taste and attention to detail.

Today, we continue their legacy by bringing generations together through food that tells stories of our land, culture, and family values.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our catering services

We provide catering services across all of Odisha including Bhubaneswar, Cuttack, Puri, Rourkela, Sambalpur, Balasore, and all other major cities and towns. For weddings, we've catered events in even remote villages across the state.

For weddings and large events (100+ guests), we recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance, especially during peak wedding season (November-February). For smaller events, 1-2 months notice is usually sufficient. Last-minute bookings may be accommodated based on availability.

Absolutely. We routinely accommodate vegetarian, vegan, Jain, gluten-free, and other dietary requirements. For religious ceremonies, we prepare strictly vegetarian food in separate utensils as per tradition. Please inform us of any special needs when booking.

All our packages include food preparation, serving staff, basic dinnerware, and buffet setup. Higher packages include live counters, premium dinnerware, and additional staff. We can also arrange for tents, chairs, tables, and decor at additional cost through our trusted partners.

We maintain the highest hygiene standards with FSSAI-certified kitchens, regular health checks for staff, gloves/hairnets during preparation, and temperature-controlled food transportation. Our ingredients are sourced fresh daily from trusted suppliers, with strict quality control at every stage.

Our Commitment to Hygiene & Food Safety

Sanitized Kitchen Facilities

Our central kitchen in Bhubaneswar is FSSAI-certified and undergoes deep cleaning daily, with all equipment sterilized after each use.

Trained Hygienic Staff

All kitchen and service staff complete rigorous hygiene training and medical check-ups every 6 months. They wear full protective gear while handling food.

Temperature Control

We maintain strict temperature controls during storage and transportation to ensure food safety from kitchen to venue.

Fresh Ingredients

We source local, seasonal ingredients from trusted suppliers, with thorough quality checks before use in any preparation.
